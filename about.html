Our Vision & Services in Detail

Toz Kitchen was founded on a simple yet profound vision: to become a leading name in culinary services and education across Nigeria and beyond, celebrated for our innovation, quality, and unwavering dedication to our community. From our base in Aba, Abia State, we've built a reputation for excellence, reaching clients in diverse locations, including Port Harcourt.

Comprehensive Catering Services:

We understand that every event is unique, and our catering services are designed to reflect that. From intimate family gatherings like child dedications to large corporate functions, Toz Kitchen provides bespoke menus tailored to your specific needs and preferences. Our expert team handles everything from menu planning and food preparation to elegant presentation and timely delivery, ensuring your event is a resounding success and truly stress-free. We pride ourselves on creating memorable dining experiences that leave a lasting impression on your guests.

Empowering Culinary Training:

Beyond serving delicious food, Toz Kitchen is passionate about nurturing the next generation of culinary talent. Our highly sought-after one-month certified chef course offers a comprehensive, hands-on training experience. Designed for aspiring chefs and food enthusiasts, the curriculum covers a wide array of cooking techniques, recipe development, and crucially, the essential business aspects of running a successful catering enterprise. We equip our students not just with cooking skills, but with the knowledge to build and manage their own culinary ventures.

Convenient Online Food Vending:

Life gets busy, but quality meals shouldn't be a compromise. Through our convenient online food vending service, Toz Kitchen brings our kitchen directly to yours. We offer a delightful selection of our signature soups, stews, and sauces, available in various litre bowl sizes for easy online purchase. Indulge in authentic traditional Nigerian dishes such as the rich and flavorful Egusi soup, classic Jollof rice, or the distinctive Ofe Achara. Our Commitment:

At Toz Kitchen, every dish we prepare and every lesson we teach is infused with our deep-seated passion for food and an unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
